Desire for Velocity

  • High Concept: Driving game where you avoid traffic to gather the candy in the road.
  • Player Experience Goals: Tension as the difficulty increase and makes it harder to progress without losing.
  • Genre: Casual game.
  • Game play: The player moves with the left and right arrows keys. Avoid the other cars to collect the candy. As you collect candy the speed and numbers of the other cars will increase making it more difficult to continue.
  • Prototype goal: Increasing difficulty as the game progresses in the form of increased speed and numbers of cars.
  • Features: Randomly spawning cars that will crash into you and slowly increasing difficulty.
  • Setting/Premise: The player had just driven through with a load of candy when it spilled out the back of his car forcing him to go back through traffic to pick it all back up while avoiding the cars.
  • Story: The player had just driven through with a load of candy when it spilled out the back of his car forcing him to go back through traffic to pick it all back up while avoiding the cars.
  • Target Audience: Gamers of all ages who want a game to play when they have a spare couple minutes.
  • Play time: A couple of minutes though potentially forever.
  • Strategies: Staying in the center of the screen will likely be the best way to play as it gives you the option of going in either direction to avoid the cars or get the candy.
